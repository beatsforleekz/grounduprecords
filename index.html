Ground Up Records was founded on the belief that great music does not need permission. We are an independent label rooted in creative freedom, championing artists who care more about identity, honesty, and longevity than chasing the algorithm.

Based in London, we work closely with a small and carefully chosen roster, offering hands-on support across recording, release strategy, distribution, and sustainable growth. Every project is personal to us, and every release is given the focus it deserves.

This is not a factory model and it is not built on volume. Ground Up is a community, shaped by trust, taste, and a shared commitment to making music that lasts.
